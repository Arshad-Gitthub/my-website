Your Roadmap

How to Launch
Arshad — Step by Step

Everything you need to go from this website to a fully live, bookable, revenue-generating business online. Follow this in order.

01
Foundation

Get a Domain Name for Arshad

Your domain is your address on the internet. Choose something clean and memorable — ideally your brand name.

Go to GoDaddy.com or Namecheap.com and search for "arshadhotel.com" or "arshadcafe.com"
Domain costs about ₹800–₹1,500 per year. Buy it for 2 years to save money.
Also grab the .in version (arshadhotel.in) if your audience is India-based.
Avoid hyphens (arshad-hotel.com looks unprofessional). Keep it short and clean.
⏱ Time: 15 minutes · Cost: ₹1,000–₹1,500/yr
02
Hosting

Choose Web Hosting to Put the Website Live

Hosting is where your website files actually live on the internet so people can visit them. Think of it as renting space on a server.

Easiest option: Use Hostinger (hostinger.com) — cheapest, fast, beginner-friendly. Plan: "Premium" at ~₹149/month.
Best performance option: Netlify.com — free for static HTML sites like this one. Just drag and drop your file. Completely free to start.
For growing businesses: Vercel.com or Render.com — free tier available, scales easily.
Since this website is a single HTML file, Netlify is the fastest way to go live today — for free.
⏱ Time: 20 minutes · Cost: Free (Netlify) or ₹149/month (Hostinger)
03
Go Live

Upload Your Website — Go Live in Minutes

This is the moment your website becomes visible to the world. If you're using Netlify, it takes under 5 minutes.

Go to netlify.com → Sign up free → Click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
Drag and drop your arshad-hotel-cafe.html file into the upload box.
Netlify gives you a free URL like arshad-hotel.netlify.app instantly.
Then go to "Domain settings" and connect your custom domain (arshadhotel.com) — Netlify walks you through it step by step.
Your site is now LIVE at arshadhotel.com 🎉
🚀 Time: 10–30 minutes · Cost: Free
04
Branding

Add Your Real Photos & Content

Right now the website uses placeholder images. To truly stun your clients, replace every placeholder with your real photos.

Hire a professional photographer for 1 day — interior, food, rooms, café. This is the single best investment you can make. Budget: ₹5,000–₹20,000.
Update phone number, email address, and physical address inside the HTML file in the "Reserve" section.
Update room prices to match your actual rates.
Replace the café menu items with your real menu.
Replace placeholder testimonials with real reviews from your early guests.
📸 Time: 1–3 days · Cost: ₹5,000–₹20,000 (photography)
05
Visibility

Set Up Google My Business — Get Found Locally

When someone searches "café near me" or "hotel in [your city]" — Google My Business is how you appear. It's free and incredibly powerful.

Go to business.google.com → Create a free account → Add "Arshad Hotel & Café"
Fill in your address, phone number, opening hours, website URL (arshadhotel.com), and upload at least 10 photos.
Verify your business (Google sends a postcard or calls your number to confirm).
Once verified, you appear on Google Maps and local search results immediately.
Ask your first 10 guests to leave a 5-star review. Reviews are your biggest trust signal.
📍 Time: 1–2 hours · Cost: Completely Free
06
Social Media

Launch on Instagram & WhatsApp Business

For a hotel and café, Instagram is your most powerful marketing tool. WhatsApp Business lets guests enquire and book directly.

Create an Instagram Business account at @arshadhotel — post 9 photos before going public to fill your grid beautifully.
Add your website link in your Instagram bio. Use the same gold/dark aesthetic from your website in your posts.
Download WhatsApp Business — add your business name, hours, and a link to your website. Use it as your booking enquiry line.
Post consistently: room photos, café shots, behind-the-scenes, morning light, guests (with permission). Aim for 4–5 posts per week at launch.
Use local hashtags: #YourCityHotel #YourCityCafe #LuxuryCafe etc. to get discovered.
📱 Time: 2–3 hours setup · Cost: Free
07
Bookings

Enable Online Bookings & Payments

Turn your website from a brochure into a revenue machine by accepting real bookings and payments online.

For café table bookings: Use Dineout for Business or EazyDiner — both free to join, they handle reservations for you.
For hotel room bookings: Register on MakeMyTrip Partner, Goibibo, and Booking.com — they list your property for free and take a small commission per booking.
For direct website payments: Create a free Razorpay or PayU account — embed a payment link so guests can pay a deposit when they fill the reservation form.
Direct bookings (via your website) = zero commission. Always push guests to book directly.
💳 Time: 1–2 days · Cost: Free to set up (% commission on bookings)
08
Growth

Launch Your Grand Opening Campaign

Make noise. The first 30 days matter the most. A well-executed launch creates momentum that carries for months.

Soft launch: Invite 20–30 friends, family, and local influencers for a free experience. Ask them to post and review. Word of mouth is everything at the start.
Influencer outreach: Contact 5–10 local food/travel Instagram influencers (10K–100K followers). Offer a complimentary stay or meal in exchange for a post and story.
Opening offer: Run a "Grand Opening — 20% off all rooms for the first 30 days" campaign via Instagram, WhatsApp, and Google.
Press: Send a short press release to local lifestyle magazines and food blogs in your city. Many will cover a beautiful new opening for free.
Paid ads: Spend ₹500–₹1,000/day on Instagram ads targeting your city with your best photo and "Now Open" message. Even a small budget gets thousands of eyes.
🎉 Time: 2–4 weeks · Budget: ₹5,000–₹20,000 for launch ads
